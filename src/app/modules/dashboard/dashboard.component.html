Customer Portal

The objective is to understand the functionalities of Sales and Distribution(SD) and Finance(FI) module.

Inquiry data

Inquiry data is a request to a company that they provide a quotation or sales information without obligation.

Sales order

It's a request from a customer to a company to deliver a quantity of products or a service at a certain time.

Delivery List

When a delivery is created, delivery scheduling, picking and packing are triggered in the system.

Payment and Aging

The payment and aging report displays the balances, sorted by date interval or by aging period definition.

Credit and Debit memos

A debit is a transaction that reduces Amounts Payable to a vendor or that reduces Amounts Receivable from a customer is a credit.

Invoice details

Invoice is a document which holds the amount to be paid back to the vendor. It was developed using SAP iRPA/Automation.

Overall Sales

Overall sales data is the overall sale happened during the particular fiscal year. The data was predicted using Machine Learnings algorithms.

Master data

Master data upload means takes the datas from excel sheet. Using file to RFC/IDOC scenario to upload customer master data.
